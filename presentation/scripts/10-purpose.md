## Script

So, the purpose — the reason all of this exists.

**We're making compute a liquid, verifiable commodity.** Something you can **price on a real market**, **prove was executed correctly**, and **settle without a middleman.** Cheaper than renting your own GPU, and verifiable in a way that no black-box API can match.

What that unlocks, for three groups:

- **For builders:** one API, pay per call, with provable execution — exactly what agentic and trust-sensitive AI needs.
- **For providers:** a way to sell spare or attested capacity per-job on a neutral venue — that's revenue, not lock-in.
- **For the ecosystem:** genuinely neutral market infrastructure — a public spot price for compute, settled on Sui.

To recap: we've built a verifiable GPU inference exchange — matched on DeepBook, verified by hardware attestation on-chain, settled on Sui with a Walrus audit trail — and it's **running on testnet today.**

Everything's on GitHub at **AreedAdmin / GIX-GPU-Inference-Exchange.**

Thank you — I'm happy to take questions, or walk through the live demo.
